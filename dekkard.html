Dekkard Chair

Seeking new shapes, the Dekkard chair was developed with gracious, fluid lines by Jaques Droz. Using 48 individual poles of spruce, neatly inserted into two pieces of solid cast iron molded into the shape that you see. The chair is meant to be a lounge chair that cradles its guest.
